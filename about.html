About Us

What is our story?

Meet Aneesah, Ellinor and Georgie: three final year Warwick students united in their search for the best places to grab a caffeinated beverage on campus. Our mission is simple: to use our final year wisdom to help you, the student on our site right now, to meet all your caffeine-related needs. You can thank us later!

We have given each coffee shop a rating out of 5 on three different categories:
- Coffee Quality- how good the coffee really is! How does it taste? Is it always the right temperature?
- Ambience - what it's like inside the cafe. Is it aesthetically pleasing (read: good for Instagram)? Is it warm? Are there enough places to sit?
- Speed - how quickly you go from order to sipping that sweet, caffeine-y goodness.
